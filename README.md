🌱 Mealo — Gentle Eating Pattern Tracking
Mealo is a mobile app designed to help busy young adults reconnect with their eating rhythm through low-pressure tracking, adaptive insights, and gentle reminders.
Instead of calorie counting or rigid meal schedules, Mealo focuses on:
nourishment awareness
eating patterns
consistency
emotional insights
sustainable habit-building
The goal is to help users better understand their relationship with nourishment without guilt or pressure.

✨ Features
🍊 Emotional Companion System
Mealo includes a soft fruit companion that reflects the user’s nourishment rhythm through subtle mood and energy changes.
The companion:
becomes sleepy during long nourishment gaps
feels energized after check-ins
changes expressions based on user rhythm
celebrates gentle progress
The experience is designed to feel supportive rather than demanding.

📊 Adaptive Insights
Mealo provides personalized reflections based on user behavior and nourishment patterns.
Examples:
“Afternoons were your strongest nourishment window.”
“You tend to nourish yourself later on busier days.”
“Morning check-ins helped your consistency.”
Insights focus on awareness rather than performance.

📅 Rhythm-Based Calendar
A soft visual calendar helps users recognize:
consistency
nourishment gaps
eating rhythms
personal patterns
The interface avoids overwhelming analytics and instead encourages reflection and understanding.

🌱 Gentle Goal Progress
Instead of aggressive streak systems, Mealo encourages sustainable consistency through:
flexible goals
soft progress indicators
low-pressure tracking
encouraging reflections

⌚ Apple Watch Support
The Apple Watch extension is designed for quick, lightweight interactions:
energy check-ins
quick nourishment logging
subtle reminders
companion reactions
The watch experience stays calm and glanceable.

🎨 Design Philosophy

Mealo is built around:
emotional safety
calm interfaces
soft gamification
spacious UI
non-clinical tracking
The app avoids:
calorie obsession
guilt-driven streaks
overwhelming dashboards
rigid meal expectations
The visual identity uses:
pastel tones
rounded layouts
soft illustrations
emotionally adaptive companion states

🛠 Built With

SwiftUI

💡 Vision
Mealo aims to create a healthier relationship with nourishment by shifting the focus from:
“Did I eat perfectly today?”
to:
“How has my eating rhythm been feeling lately?”
The app is designed to support users through awareness, reflection, and small consistent moments of care.
